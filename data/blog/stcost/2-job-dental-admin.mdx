Simple CRM (Customer Relationship Management) system for dental clinics. It helps to manage patients, appointments, treatments, invoices, payments, and more.

Full TechStack: ReactJS, MobX, TypeScript, Ant Design (antd, ant-design-pro), react-infinite-scroller, webpack, dotenv, moment.js, numeral.js, GraphQL, webpanel, react-dom, css.
