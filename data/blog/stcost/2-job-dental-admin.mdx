Simple CRM (Customer Relationship Management) system for dental clinics.

My goal was to make a CMS for managing clients, doctors, appointments, clients’ teeth and more to make dental business easier. Minimalistic design with lots of data to control.
I implemented pages with simple functionality for appointment PC, where manager could locally in browser store the info.

Funniest feature was to make array of checkboxes reassembling shape of human teeth. So doctor can mark which teeth had operation, and a description of operation

#### Full TechStack: ReactJS, MobX, TypeScript, Ant Design (antd, ant-design-pro), react-infinite-scroller, webpack, dotenv, moment.js, numeral.js, GraphQL, webpanel, react-dom, css.
