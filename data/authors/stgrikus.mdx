Hello! I'm Arthur, a 25-year-old coffee enthusiast. I'm excited to share my coffee expertise with you, particularly focusing on alternative brewing techniques:

1. Hario V60: Mastering the perfect extraction, I craft a cup that's nothing short of a masterpiece.

2. Chemex: Like a painter with a palette, I use the Chemex to brew a cup that's clean, luminous, and fragrant. Every sip is a melody in the grand concert of flavors.

3. Dotyk: With Dotyk, I sculpt a coffee experience that's as smooth as silk, with a depth of flavor that's truly indulgent.

4. Aeropress: I'm adept at tweaking the Aeropress to suit various coffee profiles, conjuring up a magical elixir in every cup.

5. Kalita Wave: The Kalita Wave is my instrument for striking the ideal harmony of sweetness and acidity, taking you on an exploratory voyage of tastes.

6. Clever: My trick with the Clever is to finesse the brewing time and temperature, ensuring every cup is a distinctive creation.

My passion for coffee is more than a hobby; it's a source of inspiration. My hands are like wands, transforming beans into potions of pure delight.

Beyond the world of coffee, I also have a deep appreciation for music, the art of photography, and the thrill of skateboarding.
